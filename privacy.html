Privacy Policy

At MyBookSeries.org, your privacy is very important to us. This page explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. What We Collect

  • Your email address, when you sign up or log in.
  • Your reading history (e.g., which chapters you’ve unlocked).
  • Payment information, only when you buy coins via PayPal.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Let you log in and access your unlocked chapters.
  • Improve our service and track reading activity.
  • Send email login links or chapter updates (if you opt in).

3. Third Parties

We use trusted services to run our site:

  • Supabase – for secure login/authentication.
  • PayPal – for processing payments securely.

We never sell your data or share it with advertisers.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please email us at:

support@mybookseries.org
